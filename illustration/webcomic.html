Astray Webcomic

Info
Astray is a webcomic collaboration between fellow student Devon Marcel and myself. The basic goals of the comic were to illustrate and design for the specific medium - in this case, online. This meant freedom to create animations and interesting dimensions that couldn't exist in a trational medium. To the right is my narrative arc within the comic. To see the full comic with mine and Devon's work visit astray.isucmp.net.
